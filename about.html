About Me

A little more than what's on the resume.

Hi, I'm Cameron. I am graduating May 2026 with degrees in Computer Science and Business Analytics from the University of San Diego. Starting August 2026 I'll be joining BlackRock's Aladdin Client Business team in Seattle as a Program Analyst.

My background sits at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and business strategy. I've interned at Cisco and AMD, managed analytics for USD's D1 baseball team, and built everything from ML platforms to a Unix shell in C. Outside of work I'm a lifelong Red Sox, Celtics, and Patriots fan, skier, runner, and weightlifter. The widgets below pull in some live data around a few of my interests — and if you haven't already, try switching the theme in the top right of the nav.

Red Sox Scores

I have always been a fan of Baseball, specifcally the Red Sox. I follow the Red Sox extremyl closely and have my entire life. I grew up watching games with my Grampa and Dad, and regardless of the outcomes, I will always be a die-hard fan. This widget pulls live game data from the MLB Stats API.

City Map

Using the search functionality above, you can find a city and view its location on the map. I have always been fascinated by geography and maps, and this widget allows me to combine that interest with my technical skills.
