About Me

A little more than what's on the resume.

I'm Cameron — a double major in Computer Science and Business Analytics at the University of San Diego, graduating May 2026. I'll be joining BlackRock's Aladdin Client Business team in Seattle as a Program Analyst starting August 2026.

My background sits at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and business strategy.
